Cestus of Disintegration

Weapon (Unarmed), unknown rarity, requires attunement by a character with proficiency in Unarmed Strikes

A scarred, metal-reinforced leather guantlet inscribed with glowing runes on the metal plates, along with a fist as the maker's mark. It was discovered in a buried research facility by explorers. Who knows what powers the guantlet is imbued with?

The Cestus provides a +2 to damage and attack rolls made with the Cestus. On a 19 or 20 on an attack roll (before modifiers and bonuses), the target suffers an additional 1d4 force damage as the magic emitted by the Cestus starts pulling the target apart from the inside. On a 1 (before modifiers or bonuses), the user suffers 1d2 force damage as the magic backlashes.
